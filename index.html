About Me

Hi there! I'm Grace Conti, a London-based showrunner and video editor with a passion for storytelling and visual artistry. With over 4 years of experience, I have contributed to the production and post-production of a diverse range of independent films, documentaries, music videos, and behind-the-scenes film content.

My journey has allowed me to work closely with filmmakers, artists, and production teams ensuring to bringing creative visions to life aiming high quality standard. My work is characterized by meticulous attention to detail, a creative and problem-solving mindset, and strong communication and collaboration skills.

Privately, I'm enthusiastic about everything that stimulates my intellect and creativity, and enjoy music, art, design and fashion.
